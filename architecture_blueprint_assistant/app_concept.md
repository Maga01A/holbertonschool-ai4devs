# Application Concept – EcoRide

## 1. Application Overview
EcoRide is a sustainable ride-sharing platform designed to minimize environmental impact. The platform connects eco-conscious riders with drivers, prioritizing electric vehicles and optimized routes to reduce carbon emissions in urban environments.

## 2. Core Features
- **Sustainability-Driven Matching**: AI-powered algorithm that prioritizes shared rides and electric vehicles (EV).
- **Eco-Impact Dashboard**: Real-time tracking of carbon footprint savings with gamified rewards and eco-points.
- **EV Prioritization**: A system that gives premium visibility and financial incentives to EV owners.
- **Corporate Commute Coordination**: Recurring ride scheduling and group coordination for enterprise employees.
- **Integrated Green Payments**: Transparent pricing with automated carbon offset purchasing options.

## 3. User Types
- **Riders**: Individuals and corporate employees seeking cost-effective, eco-friendly transportation.
- **Drivers**: Electric vehicle owners and professional drivers committed to low-emission transport.
- **Corporate Clients**: Companies managing employee commutes and tracking corporate sustainability metrics.
- **Platform Administrators**: Internal teams monitoring system performance and environmental data.

## 4. Constraints
- **Scalability**: The system must handle 50,000+ concurrent users with sub-second GPS location updates.
- **Compliance**: Full adherence to GDPR/CCPA for data privacy and PCI DSS for secure payments.
- **Mobile-First Design**: Native iOS and Android applications with basic offline functionality for trip tracking.
- **Data Accuracy**: Carbon footprint calculations must maintain a margin of error below 1%.
