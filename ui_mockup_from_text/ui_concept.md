# UI Concept - EcoRide: Sustainable Ride-Sharing

## Purpose
EcoRide is designed to provide a seamless, eco-friendly transportation experience. The UI focuses on simplifying sustainable choices by prioritizing electric vehicles (EVs) and visualizing the environmental impact of each journey in real-time.

## Users
- **Eco-Conscious Riders**: Individuals looking for affordable and green commuting options.
- **EV Drivers**: Professional and private drivers who want to monetize their electric vehicles and track their contribution to urban sustainability.
- **Corporate Managers**: Business users monitoring employee travel and corporate carbon footprint metrics.

## Core Screens
- **Rider Dashboard**: Featuring an interactive map with real-time EV availability and a "Green-Estimate" trip selector.
- **Sustainability Profile**: A personal dashboard showing carbon savings, eco-points earned, and environmental impact charts.
- **Trip History & Impact**: A detailed list of past rides with a specific breakdown of CO2 saved per trip.
- **Driver Console**: A simplified interface for drivers to manage ride requests, battery level alerts, and charging station navigation.

## Actions
- **Book Eco-Ride**: Select destination, choose EV tier, and confirm shared or private green rides.
- **Track Carbon Savings**: Real-time visualization of CO2 reduction during and after a trip.
- **Manage Eco-Rewards**: Convert accumulated eco-points into discounts or carbon offset certificates.
- **Schedule Recurrent Commutes**: Set up routine eco-friendly trips for daily office commuting.
