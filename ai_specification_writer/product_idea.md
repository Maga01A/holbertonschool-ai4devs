Product
CarpoolConnect

Vision
Enable companies to significantly reduce their carbon footprint and employee transportation costs through intelligent carpool matching, while fostering workplace community and supporting sustainability goals.

Primary Users and Needs
Corporate Employees: Need affordable, reliable daily commute options, lower travel costs, and social connection with colleagues.
Sustainability Officers: Need accurate CO2 reduction tracking, monthly/annual reporting, and progress visibility against corporate sustainability targets.
HR Managers: Need measurable employee benefit outcomes, satisfaction improvement signals, and ROI insights for mobility programs.
Facility Managers: Need to optimize limited parking capacity, reduce congestion, and allocate parking based on carpool participation.
Key Features
Smart Commute Matching: AI-based matching within a 2-mile route radius and plus/minus 30-minute schedule flexibility.
Corporate SSO Integration: Secure access via enterprise identity providers such as Azure AD and Okta.
Environmental Impact Dashboard: Real-time CO2 savings tracking with automated sustainability reporting.
Parking Optimization: Reserved parking allocation and dynamic space management for active carpool users.
Cost Savings Analytics: Individual and company-level savings insights, including ROI tracking for HR and leadership.
Differentiators
Enterprise-first design focused on corporate use cases, not general public ride-sharing.
Deep integration with internal HR, identity, and parking systems.
Compliance-forward architecture aligned with SOC 2 and GDPR requirements.
Strong emphasis on measurable business outcomes: cost reduction, emissions reduction, and employee satisfaction.
